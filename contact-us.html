Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion from your profile settings. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Contact support if you need immediate deletion.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure servers, and follow industry best practices. See our Data Protection page for detailed security information.

Do you sell my data?

No, absolutely not. We never sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is yours alone.

How do I export my data?

You can request a data export from your account settings. We'll provide your data in a portable format within 7 days.

What if I find a bug?

Please report bugs to help.rupiya@gmail.com with details about what happened. Include your device type, browser, and steps to reproduce the issue.

How often is my data backed up?

Your data is automatically backed up daily. We maintain multiple backup copies in geographically distributed locations.

Can I use Rupiya offline?

Yes! Rupiya is a Progressive Web App that works offline. Changes will sync when you reconnect to the internet.
