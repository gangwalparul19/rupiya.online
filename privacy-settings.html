What is Privacy Mode?

Privacy Mode hides all sensitive financial data (amounts, percentages, charts) while keeping the app interface fully functional. Perfect for:

  • Sharing your device with family members
  • Demonstrating app features to others
  • Taking screenshots for support
  • Protecting your financial information in public

Privacy Mode Settings

Enable Privacy Mode Hide all financial amounts and sensitive data
Hide Financial Amounts Hide income, expenses, and balance amounts
Hide Charts & Graphs Hide visual representations of financial data
Hide User Information Hide name and email address
Hide Percentages Hide savings rate and other percentages

Demo Mode for Sharing

When Privacy Mode is enabled, you can safely:

  • Share your device with family members to explore features
  • Show the app interface without revealing financial details
  • Demonstrate how to use different sections
  • Take screenshots for tutorials or support

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe in Privacy Mode?

Yes, Privacy Mode only hides data on your screen. Your actual data remains encrypted and secure on our servers. Privacy Mode is a display-only feature.

Can I use Privacy Mode on shared devices?

Yes! Privacy Mode is perfect for shared devices. When enabled, all financial amounts are hidden, allowing others to explore the app without seeing your financial details.

Does Privacy Mode affect other users?

No, Privacy Mode is stored locally on your device. It only affects your view of the app and doesn't impact other users' access to their own data.

Can I toggle Privacy Mode quickly?

You can enable Privacy Mode using the eye icon button in the header. Once enabled, the button is hidden for security - you must return to this settings page to disable Privacy Mode. This prevents others from accidentally revealing your data.
