üîç How to Find Google Sheet GID (Tab ID)

For Your Stocks Sheet (Master_Feed Tab)

Step 1: Open your stocks Google Sheet
Click here to open
Step 2: Click on the "Master_Feed" tab at the bottom
Step 3: Look at the URL in your browser's address bar
It will look like: ...#gid=123456789
Step 4: Copy the number after gid=
That's your GID!

üìù Once you have the GID:

Your environment variable should be:

GOOGLE_SHEETS_STOCKS_URL=https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1BQHeZ6KihQV-kAChsMYHScNxFlyTcfm5NL05KWlCVhE/gviz/tq?tqx=out:json&gid=YOUR_MASTER_FEED_GID

Replace YOUR_MASTER_FEED_GID with the number you found.

Example:

If the URL shows: ...#gid=987654321

Then your environment variable should be:

GOOGLE_SHEETS_STOCKS_URL=https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1BQHeZ6KihQV-kAChsMYHScNxFlyTcfm5NL05KWlCVhE/gviz/tq?tqx=out:json&gid=987654321

üîÑ Update in Vercel:

  1. Go to Vercel Dashboard ‚Üí Your Project ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Environment Variables
  2. Find GOOGLE_SHEETS_STOCKS_URL
  3. Click "Edit"
  4. Update the GID in the URL
  5. Save
  6. Redeploy your application
