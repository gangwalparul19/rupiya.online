About Rupiya

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

Our Mission

Rupiya is dedicated to empowering individuals and families to take control of their financial lives. We believe that everyone deserves access to simple, secure, and powerful tools to track, manage, and understand their investments.

Our mission is to make financial management accessible, transparent, and stress-free for everyone, regardless of their financial expertise or investment portfolio size.

What We Do

Rupiya is a comprehensive financial management application that helps you:

  • Track Investments: Monitor stocks, mutual funds, cryptocurrencies, real estate, and other assets in one place
  • Manage Portfolios: Organize your investments by type and track performance metrics
  • Monitor Expenses: Keep track of your spending and identify areas to save
  • Plan Goals: Set financial goals and track progress toward achieving them
  • Share Finances: Collaborate with family members on shared financial management
  • Get Insights: Analyze your financial data with detailed analytics and reports

Our Values

Privacy First

Your financial data is sacred. We implement end-to-end encryption with military-grade AES-256 encryption to ensure your information remains completely private. We never sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Security & Trust

We implement zero-knowledge architecture with bank-level security measures. Your data is encrypted on your device before transmission, and we cannot access your sensitive information even if we wanted to. Your trust is our most valuable asset.

Complete Data Control

You own your data completely. Export it anytime, delete it permanently, or modify it as needed. We provide full transparency about what data we collect and how it's used - no hidden agendas, no surprises.

User-Centric Design

Every feature is designed with you in mind. We listen to your feedback and continuously improve the application to meet your needs.

No Conflicts of Interest

We don't provide investment advice or recommendations. We don't benefit from your investment decisions. We're here to help you manage your finances, not to influence them.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't provide investment advice or tips - You make your own investment decisions
  • We don't sell your data - Your information is never shared with third parties for profit
  • We don't track you - We don't use invasive tracking or analytics
  • We don't manipulate your data - What you see is accurate and unbiased
  • We don't have hidden fees - Our pricing is transparent and straightforward
  • We don't act as a broker or financial institution - We're a management tool, not a financial service provider

Our Technology

Rupiya is built on modern, secure, and scalable technology with security as our top priority:

  • End-to-End Encryption: AES-256 military-grade encryption with client-side encryption
  • Zero-Knowledge Architecture: We cannot access your encrypted data even if we wanted to
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Hosted on Google Cloud Platform with enterprise-grade security
  • Database Security: Firebase Firestore with encrypted storage and secure access rules
  • Transport Security: TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit with certificate pinning
  • Authentication: Secure OAuth 2.0 with multi-factor authentication support
  • Progressive Web App: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
  • Offline Support: Encrypted local storage for offline access
  • Security Monitoring: 24/7 threat detection and automated incident response
