❓ FAQ
▼
Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes! Industry-standard encryption. Data never shared with third parties.

Q: Can I edit past transactions?

A: Yes. Click any transaction to edit or delete.

Q: Can I export my data?

A: Yes. Settings → Export Data as CSV or PDF.

Q: Is Rupiya free?

A: Yes! Completely free. No ads, no hidden charges.

Q: Can I use Rupiya offline?

A: Requires internet to sync. Some features work offline with cached data.
